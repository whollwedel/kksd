What is Kali Kempo?

It’s a modern training approach combining Shaolin Kung Fu, Shaolin Kempo, and Pekiti Tirsia Kali — blending Chinese and Filipino martial arts to create practical, well-rounded self-defense.

  • Beginner-friendly, technique-driven classes
  • Focus on awareness, de-escalation, and fitness
  • Safe training with pads and controlled drills
