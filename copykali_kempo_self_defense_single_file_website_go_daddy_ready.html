What is Kali Kempo?

It’s a modern training approach that combines the philosophies of Shaolin Kung Fu, Shaolin Kempo, and Pekiti Tirsia Kali.

Combining chinese and filipino martial arts creates a blend that allows for practical self defense, along with improving health and fitness levels.

Shaolin Kempo for versatile striking and movement, Shaolin Kung Fu for breathing and meditative techniques, and Pekiti Tirsia Kali for weapon principles that improve empty‑hand skills.

  • Beginner‑friendly, technique‑driven classes
  • Focus on awareness, de‑escalation, and fitness
  • Train safely with pads, controlled drills, and clear progressions

Safety First

We emphasize controlled drills, mutual respect, and progressive contact. Equipment is introduced as students are ready.

FAQ

Nope! Classes are beginner‑friendly with clear progressions.
