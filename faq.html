Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about what to wear, pricing, and how classes work.

Do I need prior martial arts experience?

Nope! Classes are beginner-friendly and focus on safety, control, and skill building.

What should I wear?

Comfortable athletic clothes. No uniform is required for the first month — just bring water and train barefoot on clean mats.

How do payments work?

You can pay per class ($15) or monthly ($80). There are no contracts or signup fees.

What ages do you teach?

Kids (8–13) and Teens & Adults (14+) — both classes meet Monday and Friday evenings at the Optimist Park Community Center.

Do I need to bring any gear?

No gear needed for beginners. Pads and training tools are provided. Over time, you can get your own gloves or sticks if desired.

Can I watch a class before joining?

Yes! Visitors are welcome to stop by and observe a class before participating.
